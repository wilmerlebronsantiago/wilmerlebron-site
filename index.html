About

Wilmer LeBron is a Puerto Rico–based guitarist who has recorded albums and songs with multiple projects, including Amore Ad Lunam and Neo M. His musical range spans from high-level shred guitar and modern rock to jazz, blues, salsa, and danceable Latin styles. He has shared musical spaces with internationally recognized artists and continues to refine a professional, expressive, and powerful musical identity.
